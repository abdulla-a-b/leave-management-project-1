Project A · Pilot Setup

Welcome — your roster is empty.

This is a fresh pilot deployment. Follow these four steps to go live:

  1. Connect Google Sheets — open and paste your Apps Script Web App URL.
  2. Import or add employees — go to and either bulk-import an Excel/CSV file or add joiners one by one.
  3. Employees can apply — once the roster is loaded, Part A and Part G become usable for staff.
  4. Department heads approve — Part B for approvals, Part C for payroll, Part D for welfare.
